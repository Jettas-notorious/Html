Dinner Ideas

In this recipe book we will give you a view recipe
ideas which you can implemnet in your diet that is
healthy and full of protein.You can meal prep it
and it wont break your bank.

    Ingredients for:High protein Burger
  1. Lean mince(400g)
  2. All purpose spice
  3. salt
  4. pepper
  5. Oil
  6. Buns
  7. Yum yum sauce
  8. Tomato
  9. lettuce
    Utensils
  1. tablespoon
  2. knife
  3. spatula
  4. plate
  5. pan
    High protein burgers
  1. Cut your mince into two balls
  2. Spice each ball with the all purpose spice and the salt and pepper
  3. Preheat your pan and when it is hot enough throw in your oil
  4. Bake your patties for 2-3 minutes on each side
  5. Cut your bun through the middle
  6. add your yum yum sauce on both sides
  7. Add your lettuce, tomato and your patties
  8. Enjoy
    Ingredients for:chicken wraps
  1. 400g of chicken breast
  2. Tortillas
  3. shredded cheese
  4. yum yum sauce
  5. oil
  6. all purpose spice
  7. lettuce(shredded)
  8. butter
    Utensils
  1. tablespoon
  2. pan
  3. knife
    Chicken wrap
  1. Cut your chicken breast into smaller pieces
  2. Spice your chicken with the all purpose spice
  3. heat your pan and add 3 tablespoons of oil
  4. Cook your chicken ontil fully cooked
  5. Warm torillas in the microwave
  6. Add yum yum sauce to the wrap
  7. Add lettuce
  8. Add cooked chicken
  9. Add cheese
  10. Place butter in the pan and place your wraps in the pan and remove when golden
  11. Enjoy
